Pasta Recipe

Description of Recipe

Pasta is a classic italian dish

Pasta is quick easy and delicious

Ingredients

  • Penne Pasta
  • Water
  • Salt
  • 1 jar of tomato pasta sauce

Steps

  1. Put water in a pot on the stove on high heat
  2. Boil the water
  3. Add salt
  4. Put pasta in water for 10 minutes
  5. Strain pasta
  6. Toss pasta in tomato sauce and serve!
