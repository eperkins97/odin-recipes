Beef Tacos

/>

Description of Recipe

Beef tacos are a delicious and easy-to-make dish that is perfect for any occasion. They are made with seasoned ground beef, fresh toppings, and warm tortillas.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 8 small tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup shredded cheese

    • Steps

    1. Cook beef with taco seasoning in a skillet.
    2. Warm toritllas in a pan or microwave
    3. Assemble tacos with beef, lettuce, tomatoes and cheese
    4. Serve and enjoy!
