Greek Salad

Description of the recipe

Greek Salad is a refreshing and healthy dish made with fresh vegetables, olives, and feta cheese. It's perfect as a side dish or a light meal.

Ingredients

  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Red Onion
  • Kalamata Olives
  • Feta Cheese
  • Olive Oil
  • Red Wine Vinegar
  • Oregano

Steps

  1. Chop all vegetables and combine in a bowl.
  2. Add olives and crumbled feta.
  3. Whisk olive oil, vinegar, and oregano to make dressing
  4. Pour dressing over salad and toss gently
